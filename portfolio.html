Data Transformation

Problem: Data was very dirty with inconsistent or inaccurate values, redundant fields, and missing entries.

Solution: Data transformation with Python, Excel, and generative AI tools.

Outcome: Increased time efficiency by ~50%, reduced missing data, and delivered a cleansed, structured dataset. Established a repeatable process for future enhancements.

Process Optimization

Problem: Data workflow was highly manual, requiring employees to repeatedly copy and paste information. This led to inefficiencies.

Solution: Automation of key parts of the workflow with Excel, Sheets, Python, and generative AI to eliminate repetitive tasks and improve efficiency.

Outcome: Reduced manual processing time by over 80%. Generated more than $100 in direct time savings. Proved a scalable process that maximizes efficiency.

Data Insights

Problem: A business needed structured insights but lacked the expertise to analyze trends, patterns, and key performance indicators.

Solution: Exploratory data analysis techniques, including segmentation, visualization, and trend detection, applied using tools such as Excel and PowerPoint.

Outcome: Identified key trends that improved decision-making. Segmented data and provided structured insights to support automation and deeper analytics.

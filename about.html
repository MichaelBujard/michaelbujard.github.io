About Me

I specialize in turning messy data into structured, reliable information that helps businesses streamline workflows, reduce errors, and gain actionable insights. With a background in data transformation and workflow optimization, I work with individuals and organizations to make their data more efficient, accurate, and automation-ready.

My journey started with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Dallas, where I developed both technical expertise and a problem-solving mindset. Since then, I’ve worked with business data, automation, and e-commerce operations, ensuring that product information and other datasets are structured for usability and business insights.

My experience spans data transformation, workflow improvement, and process optimization, with a strong focus on preparing data for future automation. Whether you need help standardizing raw datasets, optimizing business processes, or ensuring data integrity, I provide practical solutions to keep your operations running efficiently.

Outside of work, I enjoy keeping up with trends in science, technology, and engineering, having meaningful conversations, and playing rugby.
